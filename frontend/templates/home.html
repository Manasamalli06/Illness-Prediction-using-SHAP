About AI HealthGuard

The Technology

Our system leverages Optimized XGBoost, a state-of-the-art gradient boosting algorithm specifically tuned for tabular clinical data. By processing hundreds of patient parameters, the model identifies complex non-linear patterns that traditional methods might miss.

Explainability (SHAP)

We believe AI should be a "glass box," not a black box. Integrating SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations) allows us to show exactly which symptoms or metrics influenced each prediction, providing clinicians with actionable insights.

Clinical Precision

Trained on validated medical datasets, the system focuses on high precision for critical outcomes. While it acts as a decision-support tool, it ensures that high-risk cases are flagged with maximum reliability for further professional review.
